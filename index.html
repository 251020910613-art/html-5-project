About the Photographer

Chasing light across every continent — one frame at a time.

Hello, I'm Duong

I'm a nature and travel photographer based in San Francisco, CA. For the past decade I've wandered deserts, rainforests, and mountain ranges with a camera, searching for the brief window of perfect light.

My work has appeared in National Geographic Traveler, Outdoor Photographer, and various gallery exhibitions across North America and Europe.

Every photograph is a collaboration with nature — I just show up and try not to miss it.

Gear & Approach

Camera Bodies

Sony A7R V & Nikon Z9 for landscapes and wildlife. Lightweight mirrorless for hiking.

Lenses

24–70mm f/2.8 for versatility. 500mm telephoto for wildlife. 14mm ultra-wide for grand vistas.

Post-Processing

Adobe Lightroom for culling and color grading. Photoshop for composites and fine detail work.

Philosophy

Minimal editing — the magic happens in the field, not on the screen. Natural light always wins.
